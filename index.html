About Cosimino...

His background

Cosimino was born in Supersano, in the picturesque countryside of Salento, South Italy. He started creating wrought-iron paintings and handrails for his house and ended up forging new and diverse artworks.

He acquired his skills by working as a plumber, electrician, and carpenter for all his life. He is now acknowledged as the local Geppetto.

Local recognition

Cosimino's artworks have been widely welcomed by the local community. As he rides his bike every day, he easily runs into farmers that offer him to bring some wood back to life. This way, Cosimino's commitment never failed to convert wild pieces of trunks into beautiful and sustainable works. Among his works, one can find the classics, homeware, toys, iron paintings, as well as brain teasers.

Press and institutions

Cosimino's story can be found on a local newspapers Il Nostro Giornale, which dedicated an article to his activity. He is also know for having donated an iron painting to the local traffic wardens, depicting their Patron Saint Sebastian.
